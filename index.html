02
Documentation & Data Collection

A structured checklist is shared. All relevant financial records, statutory documents and prior filings are collected, validated and organised for accurate processing.

Filings, reports, payroll runs or compliance tasks are processed against statutory timelines. A two-stage review ensures accuracy before any submission or delivery.
